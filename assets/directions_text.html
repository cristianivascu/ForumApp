I From the Airport

1.1 By Bus

Airlink is the quickest way to travel between the airport and the city centre, however this route is not direct.

  • Take from: airport, Stances 3 and 4
  • Stop at: Waverley Bridge, outside Edinburgh Waverley Train Station. Ask the driver to give you a call when the bus reaches the station (press to see how to reach The Forum from here);
  • Frequency: Every 10 minutes
  • Duration: 30 minutes
  • Availability: 7 days a week
  • Ticketing: A single adult ticket on Airlink is £3.50. A return adult ticket is £6.00. Tickets can be bought on board the bus. City singles and Day tickets are not valid on Airlink.

Service 35 offers a direct link from the airport through the heart of the University's Central Area. Note that this services takes 30 minutes longer to travel between the city centre and the airport, and it may actually be faster to travel by Airlink.

  • Take from: airport, Stance 6
  • Stop at: Forrest Road. Ask the driver to give you a call when the bus reaches Forrest Road
  • Frequency: Every 15 minutes
  • Duration: 1 hour
  • Availability: 7 days a week
  • Ticketing: A single adult ticket costs £1.30. Tickets can be bought on board the bus. The correct change is required to pay the fare.

Press the 'Menu' button of your phone to see the location of The Forum relative to Forrest Road. Please note that it will take no longer than a 5-minute walk to reach the Forum from here. You can also ask any pedestrian about the location of the Central University Campus.

Service N22 is an alternative if you arrive late at night.

  • Take from: airport, Stances 3 and 4
  • Stops at: Waverley Steps, outside Edinburgh Waverley Train Station
  • Frequency: Every 30 minutes
  • Duration: Approximately 30 minutes
  • Days: 7 days a week
  • Ticketing: An adult fare is £3.00 for all night.

1.2 By Taxi

If you would like to take a taxi, you can follow the signs inside the airport which will lead you to the taxi booking centre. You can also call Central Taxis on 0131 229 2468 if you would like to book a Taxi by yourself. The price of the trip is approximately £20.00 and the trip takes about 20 minutes. The exact address of the Forum is 10 Crichton Street, EH8 9AB. The major cross streets are Potterrow and West Nicolson Street, and it is located close to the Mosque and Islamic Centre. The Informatics Forum is a modern five storey white building, as seen in the background picture of this application.

II From the Waverley Train Station

2.1 On Foot

There are different paths you can choose to reach the Forum on foot. The recommended one is slightly longer, but it has certain advantages: it makes traveling with a trolley easier, the streets are well lit and it is easier and safer to travel at night, the streets are large and with few intersections, so it is less likely to get lost. Overall, it should take about 20 minutes to reach the Forum.

  1. Exit the Train Station on Princess Street
  2. Turn right and then walk straight, until the first intersection
  3. Turn right in the intersection and go straight ahead. You should be crossing a large bridge in a few moments (North Bridge)
  4. Keep walking and after a few minutes you will reach another intersection, with High Street.
  5. North Bridge will end, but you will keep walking forward on South Bridge
  6. At some point, South Bridge will end and it will continue with Nicolson Street
  7. Keep walking on Nicolson Street, and you will soon see a square on the right with a small garden in the middle
  8. This is Nicolson Square, and here you will have to turn right. At this point, you might be able to see the Forum
  9. After turning right, go forward until you reach Potterow. You should now clearly see the back of the Forum, a modern five storey white building
  10. Cross the street and circle the forum through the left. At the corner of the building, turn right. You should now see the main entrance.

By Bus

Service 42 offers a direct link from the train station to the Forum.

  • Take from: Waverley Bridge, outside Edinburgh Waverley Train Station.
  • Stop at: The Mosque. Ask the driver to give you a call when the bus reaches the Mosque. You should see the Forum from here, opposite to the bus stop.
  • Frequency: Every 20 minutes
  • Duration: 15 minutes
  • Availability: 7 days a week
  • Ticketing: A single adult ticket costs £1.30. Tickets can be bought on board the bus. The correct change is required to pay the fare.
